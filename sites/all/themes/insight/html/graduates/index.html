What is your background? What were you doing before coming to Insight School of Interior Design?

Natasza Minasiewicz is a Polish qualified Architect and Interior Designer. She has a broad range of experience in both architecture and interior design, having qualified as an architect and then going on to specialize in interiors.

Her work has focused on interior architecture and fitting out of high-end retail, such as Balmain and Jimmy Choo, corporate spaces as well as luxury residential interiors.

Natasza is also an accomplished photographer and her portfolio of clients include Dior, Lane Crawford and LADYGUNN Magazine.
