Project showcase

Commercial certificate client project

Background

Renowned Parisian fashion brand BA&SH came to Insight School for help with their retail expansion in HK and broader Asia.

Recently acquired by LMVH, BA&SH enlisted the help of Insight School Diploma students to translate and adapt their strong European retail design to the local Asian markets.

Project brief

  • Adapt the existing European retail store design to better meet the Asian consumer's needs and tastes

  • Develop a store layout that is more lively and better lit

  • Redesign and increase customer seating throughout the store

  • Redesign the accessories visual merchandising

  • Create a new window display for Chinese new year
